About me

Hi! I am Augus, a designer, developer & creator.

I speak German, English, Spanish, and a little Norwegian, and love (read:LOVE) arts and music. I play the guitar, piano, drums, and just beginning to learn the saxophone! Also, sports are fun, too! Volleyball is my jam.

Professionally, I excell in design (UX design, graphic design, 3D modelling), Web and App Development, as well as content creation through photography & videography (incl. with drones), and editing.
