Christoph Vornehm

Passionate nature photographer and adventurer.

Nature

My inspiration

Being out in nature brings me back to myself. There's something real and grounding about watching the weather change or standing quietly as the light fades in the woods. Even in the wildest moments—when the wind howls or rain drives everything into hiding—I feel connected, like I’ve stepped into something honest. That’s what draws me to photography: trying to hold onto those fleeting, unspoken moments.

Adventures

My Work

The perfect way to capture nature at its untouched beauty, is by immersing myself in it, staying unrecognized while hiking and camping in the wilderness for days at a time. No phone, no connection, only me, a compass,and my camera.
